The Nansen Environmental and Remote Sensing Center (NERSC) in Bergen, Norway, is seeking a researcher with expertise in marine ecosystem assessment, optical remote sensing, and machine learning.

The role involves developing solutions for monitoring coastal environments and predicting marine hazards such as harmful algal blooms, high biomass blooms, salmon lice, and Vibrio spp.

**Deadline:** December 7, 2025.
