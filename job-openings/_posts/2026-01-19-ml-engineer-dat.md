Dimensions Agri Technologies (DAT) provides smart data-driven camera systems for precise pesticide spraying. This technology reduces chemical use by half, and improves crop health and farmer economics. DAT is in a commercial growth phase, and works with clients across Europe. 

You will benefit from experience with AI-models for image classification (2+ years) and interest for computer vision. This position provides opportunities for field work and research cooperation within Europe. This permanent position is based in Ski, Norway. Working languages are English and Norwegian. 

Key responsibilities include:
* End-to-end model pipeline development 
* Development and optimization of neural network models for real-time inference on Jetson devices (quantization/TensorRT integration/latency profiling)
* Large dataset (open/proprietary) management and annotation
