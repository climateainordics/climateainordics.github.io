A fully-funded 4-year PhD position is available starting June 01, 2026, in Erlangen, Germany, as part of the M3OCCA program.

The research topic involves developing deep learning methods to improve robustness and adaptability in glacier surface and bedrock detection from radargrams. The research will focus on continual learning for incremental adaptation to new data and foundation models for robust generalization across diverse glaciers, sensors, and environments.
