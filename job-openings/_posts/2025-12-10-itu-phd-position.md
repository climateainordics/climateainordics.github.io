The IT University (ITU) of Copenhagen is hiring a PhD student within the project “ClimateAdapt: Human adaptability to climate change”, which
is funded by the Independent Research Fund Denmark. Its goal is to use high-resolution human mobility data in combination with natural
experiments to uncover how changes in climate affect human behaviors across different demographics.
ClimateAdapt will provide data-driven recommendations to policy makers on how to design effective responses to climate change. 

**Deadline:** January 11, 2026.
