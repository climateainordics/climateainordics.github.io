Welcome to the January edition of the Climate AI Nordics Newsletter!

Since launching in October 2024, our community has grown to 222 members across the Nordic region and 86 international supporters. Together, we connect researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and climate action—spanning mitigation, adaptation, and environmental monitoring.

If you know colleagues in academia, public agencies, or industry who share these interests, invite them to join us at climateainordics.com/join.

Inside this edition: Catch up on the AICC workshop talks, explore our featured paper on particle number concentrations, and meet our January Featured Member.
